About

A short professional introduction that works well for conference visitors scanning your poster QR code.

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Antwerp and the Industrial Vision Lab (InViLab), supported by the Research Foundation Flanders (FWO). My research focuses on improving breast reconstruction workflows by combining dynamic infrared thermography, computational modeling, and data-driven methods. I am especially interested in how measurement technologies and AI can contribute to clinically relevant, non-invasive decision support.
